About us

Two decades of trusted machinery imports.

Unique Press has supplied Indian printers with quality pre-owned offset and post-press machinery for over twenty years. We import directly from Japan, where machines are meticulously maintained, and bring them to India inspected, serviced and ready to run.

Our own engineers handle everything from inspection to installation — so you deal with one team, from enquiry to first print.
